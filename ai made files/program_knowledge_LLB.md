## Prerequisite Rules

### Direct Prerequisites:
- **LLB101** → LLB103, LLB104
- **LLB102** → LLB202, LLB206
- **LLB103** → LLB304, LLB305
- **LLB202** → LLB302, LLB303
- **LLB404** → LLB407

### Prerequisite Validation:
- Students must complete prerequisite before dependent course
- Prerequisite violations should be flagged but not block graduation (administrative issue)
- Dissertation (LLB407) strictly requires LLB404

---

## Year Progression Rules

### Year 1 Requirements:
- Must complete all GED Group 1 courses by end of Year 1
- Must complete LLB101 and LLB102 by end of Year 1
- Cannot proceed to Year 2 core courses without Year 1 foundation

### Year 2 Requirements:
- Must complete LLB101 before taking LLB103, LLB104
- Must complete LLB102 before taking LLB202, LLB206
- Should complete Year 2 core before Year 3

### Year 3 Requirements:
- Must complete LLB202 before taking LLB302, LLB303
- Must complete LLB103 before taking LLB304, LLB305
- Can start taking electives (recommended: 4 electives in Year 3)

### Year 4 Requirements:
- Must complete at least 100 credits before LLB407 (Dissertation)
- Must complete LLB404 (Legal Research) before LLB407
- LLB407 should be taken in final semester
- Remaining 4 electives should be completed in Year 4

## Academic Policies (LL.B Program)

### Minimum Requirements:
- **Total Credits**: 130
- **Minimum CGPA**: 2.00
- **Core Courses**: All 27 required
- **Dissertation**: LLB407 required
- **GED**: 25 credits (Group 1 + Group 2)
- **Electives**: 8 courses (24 credits)

### Graduation Requirements:
1. ✅ Complete 130 credits
2. ✅ Achieve CGPA ≥ 2.00
3. ✅ Complete all GED requirements (Group 1 + 2)
4. ✅ Complete all 27 core courses
5. ✅ Complete 8 elective courses
6. ✅ Complete LLB407 (Dissertation)
7. ✅ No probation status (CGPA ≥ 2.00)
8. ✅ All prerequisite requirements met

### Probation Rules:
- CGPA < 2.00 → Probation
- Maximum 3 semesters on probation
- Must raise CGPA to 2.00 within 3 semesters
- Dismissed after 3 semesters if CGPA still < 2.00

### Course Exclusion Rules:
- Can exclude courses after 80% credits (104 credits)
- Cannot exclude core courses
- Cannot exclude to avoid probation

### Retake Policy:
- Can retake any course with grade B or lower
- Best grade counts for CGPA
- Maximum 3 attempts per course
- 4th attempt requires Vice-Chancellor approval

## Edge Cases & Flags

### ⚠️ Flag 1: Missing Dissertation
- **Condition**: All requirements met except LLB407
- **Action**: Cannot graduate, must complete LLB407
- **Message**: "CRITICAL: Law Dissertation (LLB407) is required for graduation"

### ⚠️ Flag 2: Dissertation Prerequisites
- **Condition**: Attempting LLB407 without prerequisites
- **Action**: Flag prerequisite violation
- **Message**: "Cannot take LLB407 without completing LLB404 and 100 credits"

### ⚠️ Flag 3: GED Group 2 Violation
- **Condition**: < 3 courses or > 3 courses from Group 2
- **Action**: Flag GED deficiency
- **Message**: "GED Group 2: Must select exactly 3 courses (currently: X)"

### ⚠️ Flag 4: GED Group 1 Incomplete
- **Condition**: Missing courses from Group 1
- **Action**: Flag GED deficiency
- **Message**: "GED Group 1: Missing required course(s): [list]"

### ⚠️ Flag 5: Elective Deficiency
- **Condition**: < 8 elective courses
- **Action**: Flag elective requirement
- **Message**: "Electives: Need 8 courses, currently have X (missing Y)"

### ⚠️ Flag 6: Probation Status
- **Condition**: CGPA < 2.00
- **Action**: Cannot graduate
- **Message**: "Cannot graduate on probation (CGPA: X.XX < 2.00)"
